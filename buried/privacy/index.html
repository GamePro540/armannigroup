Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

The Buried mobile application is developed and operated by an independent developer. This privacy policy explains how your data is handled when you use the app. Buried is built with a privacy-first, on-device architecture.

1. Zero Data Collection

Buried operates entirely on your device. The developer does not collect, store, or have access to any of your data. All photos, videos, text, and GPS coordinates you save inside a time capsule remain strictly on your physical iPhone.

2. Location Services

Buried is a location-locked application and requires access to your device's GPS to function.

  • The developer does not track your movements in the background.
  • Your location is not stored on any external servers.
  • Your current location is only checked locally on your device against the locked coordinates when you attempt to open a capsule.

3. Camera and Media Access

Buried requires access to your camera and photo library only so you can attach media to your time capsules. The app does not scan your library or access media you do not explicitly attach.

4. Data Deletion and Backups

Because your data is stored only on your device, deleting the Buried app will permanently delete all your time capsules. If you use iCloud device backups, your capsules may be included according to your Apple ID backup settings. The developer cannot recover lost capsules.

5. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Since the developer does not collect email addresses, significant changes will be indicated by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

6. Contact

If you have questions about privacy or how your data is handled, please use the support/contact link on this website.
