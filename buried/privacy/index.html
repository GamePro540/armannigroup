Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Armanni Group ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Buried mobile application. We built Buried with a privacy-first, on-device architecture. This policy explains how your data is handled.

1. Zero Data Collection

Buried operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your data. All photos, videos, text, and GPS coordinates you save inside a time capsule remain strictly on your physical iPhone.

2. Location Services

Buried is a location-locked application and requires access to your device's GPS to function.

  • We do not track your movements in the background.
  • We do not store your location history on any external servers.
  • Your current location is only checked locally against the locked coordinates on your device when you attempt to open a capsule.

3. Camera and Media Access

Buried requires access to your camera and photo library solely to allow you to attach media to your time capsules. We do not scan your library or access media you do not explicitly attach.

4. Data Deletion and Backups

Because your data lives exclusively on your device, deleting the Buried app will permanently delete all your time capsules. If you use iCloud device backups, your capsules may be backed up according to your personal Apple ID settings. We cannot recover lost capsules for you.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Since we do not collect email addresses, we will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us via the support link on our website.
